Why Walmart? Retail data is notoriously complex because it’s affected by everything: holidays, weather, and even fuel prices. My task was to take historical sales data and find the "signals" in the noise. I spent weeks understanding variables like the Consumer Price Index (CPI) and Unemployment rates to see how they impact weekly revenue.
